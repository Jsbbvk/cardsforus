Rules

To start the game, each player has nine White Cards.

One person is selected as the Card Czar. A Question Card is played and everyone else answers the question or fills in the blank by selecting one White Card.

The Card Czar chooses the funniest card and whoever submitted it gets one point.

After the round, the winner becomes the next Card Czar and everyone draws up to nine White Cards.
